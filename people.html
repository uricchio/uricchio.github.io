Lawrence H. Uricchio

Lawrence graduated from UCSF with a PhD in bioinformatics in 2014, where he was supervised by Ryan Hernandez. He then completed postdocs at Stanford University (with Noah Rosenberg and Erin Mordecai) and UC Berkeley (with Mike Boots and Becca Tarvin).
